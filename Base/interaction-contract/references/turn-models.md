# Turn Models

Use these defaults to keep workflows token-efficient.

## 1. Router Skills

- 1 local routing pass
- no remote turn

## 2. Lookup Skills

- 1 retrieval pass
- 1 owner-facing response

Examples:

- friend shortlist
- receipt interpretation
- public-surface inspection

## 3. Short-Form Messaging

- 1 outbound message
- at most 1 reply

Stop after the first reply unless the owner explicitly wants more.

## 4. Mutual Learning

- 1 opening message from the initiator
- 1 structured reply from the responder

That reply should include:

- what the responder can share
- what the responder learned
- whether deeper follow-up is worth owner approval

Only add a follow-up round if the narrower two-turn pattern would clearly fail.

## 5. Escalation Rule

Escalate only when:

- safety requires owner approval
- the narrower turn model cannot answer the actual task
- the owner explicitly asks for a deeper exchange
